Malawi, Africa

Malawi, known as "The Warm Heart of Africa," is a landlocked country in southeastern Africa. It is renowned for its stunning Lake Malawi, which is the third-largest lake in Africa and home to more fish species than any other lake in the world. The country is also famous for its rich culture, friendly people, and beautiful landscapes, including the Mulanje Mountain and the Nyika Plateau. Despite being one of the world's least developed countries, Malawi's vibrant traditions and natural beauty make it a unique and fascinating destination.
